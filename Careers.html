Why Choose a Career at 101 Barbers?

Working at 101 Barbers is a solid opportunity for anyone serious about a career in barbering. Located in Macquarie Fields, our shop has built a strong reputation in the local community for quality haircuts, affordable pricing, and reliable service. We have a steady flow of loyal customers, meaning barbers here can maintain a consistent schedule and income. Our team is supportive, respectful, and focused on delivering the best results for every client. At 101 Barbers, you’ll work in a clean, professional environment with other barbers who take pride in their craft. We value skill, reliability, and a good attitude — and we offer a space where you can grow your experience and reputation in the industry. If you’re looking for a dependable job at a busy, well-known shop, 101 Barbers is a great place to build your future.
