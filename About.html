About Us

◆◆◆

Nestled right in the heart of Macquarie Fields, 101 Barbers is proud to be a locally owned and operated barbershop that’s deeply connected to the community we serve. We're not a franchise or a chain — we’re your neighbourhood barbers, dedicated to keeping locals looking sharp while building long-lasting relationships with every client who walks through our door.

Our mission goes beyond just cutting hair. We aim to create a space where people from all walks of life can come together, relax, and enjoy a high-quality grooming experience without breaking the bank. At 101 Barbers, we believe looking fresh shouldn't cost a fortune — that's why we’ve made it a priority to keep our prices low and our service quality high.

Whether you're after a fade, a classic cut, beard grooming, or a brand-new style, our experienced barbers are here to deliver precision and style tailored just for you. Every cut is crafted with care, and we take the time to listen to exactly what you want — because we believe your look should reflect your personality.

Our barbershop isn’t just a place to get a haircut — it’s a hub for connection. From kids to seniors, new customers to returning regulars, we welcome everyone with a friendly atmosphere and a genuine vibe. The shop is a reflection of the Macquarie Fields community: diverse, down-to-earth, and full of pride.

So if you’re looking for a local barbershop that feels like home, offers unbeatable prices, and delivers sharp, stylish cuts every time, 101 Barbers is the spot for you. Come in today and experience the community spirit — and the fresh look — that sets us apart.
